## Frequently Asked Questions

### Billing & Payments

**Q: Do I need to enter a credit card for the Free plan?**
A: No! The Free plan is completely free with no credit card required. Upgrade to Pro whenever you're ready.

**Q: Can I change plans anytime?**
A: Yes! Upgrade or downgrade your plan at any time. Changes take effect immediately, and we'll prorate any billing accordingly.

**Q: What payment methods do you accept?**
A: We accept all major credit cards (Visa, Mastercard, American Express), PayPal, and wire transfers for Enterprise plans.

**Q: Is there a free trial for Pro?**
A: Yes! All new Pro signups get 14 days free before billing starts.

### Features & Limitations

**Q: What happens to my files if I downgrade from Pro to Free?**
A: If you're over the Free tier limits, you can still access and download your files, but you won't be able to create new files until you're within limits.

**Q: Can I use Codexa for commercial projects on the Free plan?**
A: Yes, the Free plan is suitable for commercial use as well. For teams or larger projects, we recommend Pro or Enterprise.

**Q: How many AI messages can I use?**
A: Free users get 50 messages/month. Pro and Enterprise users get unlimited messages.

### Security & Privacy

**Q: Is my API key secure?**
A: Yes! Your OpenAI API key is stored locally in your browser using encrypted storage. We never see or store your keys on our servers.

**Q: Can I use Codexa on-premise?**
A: On-premise deployment is available with our Enterprise plan. Contact sales@codexa.ai for details.

**Q: Do you store my code?**
A: No. Your code is stored locally in your browser. We don't have access to your code unless you explicitly choose to share it.

### Technical Questions

**Q: What's included in the API access for Enterprise?**
A: Enterprise customers get webhook access and API endpoints to integrate Codexa with your internal tools.

**Q: Is there an offline mode?**
A: Offline mode is included in the Enterprise plan. Free and Pro users can use the editor while disconnected, but AI features require an internet connection.
